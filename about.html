About Us

Pointless Polygons is a small independent game studio focused on experimental and intentionally minimal game concepts. The studio was created as part of a university project and does not currently operate as a commercial entity.

Our Brands

As part of this project, a distinct trademark form was designed.

1. Word Mark: “An Unremarkable Bike Game”

This word mark represents the title of the game itself. The intentionally descriptive and self-deprecating wording was chosen to avoid confusion with existing commercial titles and to clearly communicate the tone of the game.

The wording does not reference any existing brands, franchises, or protected titles and is therefore unlikely to cause confusion in the marketplace.

Relevant Nice Class:
Class 9 – Computer software and video game software

Trademark Law Considerations

Care was taken to avoid trademark infringement by ensuring:

  • Original wording without references to existing brands
  • Abstract and non-descriptive imagery
  • Neutral color choices without strong brand associations
  • Clear separation from existing commercial offerings

No trademark registrations currently exist for this project. All brands are used solely within the context of this academic exercise.
